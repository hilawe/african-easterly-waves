# Derived per-trough tables

These tables are regenerated by a single driver script in the code archive
(`scripts/build_deposit.py`, default invocation, seed 0). Every statistic quoted in the
paper appears exactly once in `canonical_numbers.csv`; the per-trough, per-case, and
per-parcel tables carry the underlying values.

Clock convention. All times are relative to trough passage at the sampling meridian. The
environment sample sits at -24 h and the 48 h back-trajectories extend the record to
-72 h. Column suffixes `m24` .. `m72` are passage-relative hours.

Class labels. MCS-active and MCS-quiet are the upper and lower terciles of the forward
mesoscale-convective response within each 10-degree longitude by calendar-month cell
(minimum 30 troughs per cell). Activity refers to continental convection within 24 h of
trough passage, never to tropical cyclogenesis.

Tiers. `dev` is the 2000-2004 design sample, `heldout` the 20 untouched seasons
(1983-1999 and 2005-2007), `pooled` the primary 25-season record (1983-2007).

## canonical_numbers.csv

One row per reported statistic.

- `tier`; `level` (700, 850, or empty for the column/shear family); `statistic`;
  `time_rel_h` (passage-relative hours); `unit`; `kind` (contrast or point)
- `n_quiet`, `n_active` (observations); `clusters_quiet`, `clusters_active` (waves)
- `mean_quiet`, `mean_active`, `diff` (active minus quiet), `ci_lo`, `ci_hi`
  (wave-cluster bootstrap 95 percent interval), `significant`
- `note` (estimator details; box placement, conditionality, threshold)

## troughs_<tier>.csv

Every JAS corridor trough observation (5-20 N, 30 W to 40 E): `traj_id` (wave), `time`,
`lat`, `lon`, `year`, `month`, `response` (forward CS-245 count), `label`.

## cases_<tier>_<level>.csv

One row per trough observation in the stratified split. Identifiers as above, plus:

- `box_rh_m24`: the Eulerian box sample
- `rh_m24`..`rh_m72` and `thetae_m24`..`thetae_m72`: along-inflow case means
- `mix_*` and `T_*`: the vapor/temperature decomposition
- `frac_south`..`frac_local`: origin-sector parcel fractions
- `dlat_origin`: meridional displacement of the origin, degrees
- `exceed_p70`: the -72 h inflow sample exceeds the tier's pooled 70th percentile
- `antecedent`: prior-day CS-245 count (700 hPa table only)

## parcels_<tier>_<level>.csv

One row per back-trajectory parcel (9 per case): `case`, `parcel`, `traj_id`,
`seed_time`, `seed_lat`, `seed_lon`, `origin_lat`, `origin_lon` (position at -72 h),
`sector` (displacement-based origin class; `lost` where the parcel left the domain or
record).
